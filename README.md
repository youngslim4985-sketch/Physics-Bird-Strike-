Physics Bird Strike™

Aviation Impact Simulation & Engineering Analysis

«Model Impact. Improve Safety. Advance Engineering.»

Physics Bird Strike™ is a physics-based simulation platform designed to study bird strike scenarios for educational, engineering, and aviation safety research.

The project explores impact mechanics, collision modeling, and structural response through computational simulation and visualization, helping developers, researchers, and students better understand the physical principles involved in bird strike events.

---

Overview

Bird strikes are a recognized challenge in aviation, affecting aircraft, airports, and wildlife management worldwide.

Physics Bird Strike provides a framework for modeling impact scenarios, visualizing simulation results, and supporting engineering analysis of collision events.

The project is intended for:

- Engineering education
- Simulation research
- Physics demonstrations
- Aviation safety studies
- Software development

---

Mission

Build an accessible simulation platform that helps engineers, researchers, and students better understand impact physics and aviation safety concepts through computational modeling.
